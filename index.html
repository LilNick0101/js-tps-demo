How to Play

🎮 Controls

  • W/A/S/D - Move forward, left, backward, right
  • Space - Jump, press in mid air for a double jump
  • Shift - Dash in the current movement direction
  • Mouse - Look around
  • Left Click (Hold) - Shoot

🎯 Gamemodes

  • Team Deathmatch: Teams compete for battlefield supremacy to get the most kills within a time limit or a score limit.
  • Free-for-All: Every player for themselves in a chaotic battle to reach the highest kill count.
  • Capture the Flag: Teams compete to capture the enemy's flag and return it to their base while defending their own flag.
  • King of the Hill: Teams fight to control a central specific point on the map, earning points for holding it over time.
  • Bounty Hunter: Teams earn money as they eliminate opponents and complete objectives, they need to secure the money on the bank otherwise other players can steal it.
  • Survival/Team Survival: Players have limited lives, and the last player or team standing wins.

💀 Killstreaks

  • 2 Kills - Double Kill
  • 3 Kills - Triple Kill
  • 4 Kills - Mega Kill
  • 5 Kills - Killing Spree
  • 8 Kills - Monster Kill
  • 11+ Kills - UNSTOPPABLE
  • 20+ Kills - GODLIKE

💡 Tips

  • Keep moving! If you stand still, you're an easy target
  • Watch your health bar at the bottom of the screen
  • The kill feed (top right) shows recent eliminations
  • You'll respawn after death - don't give up!
  • Aim is not everything, use your abilities to dominate!
  • Watch out for important buffs like the QUAD DAMAGE! They can change the tide of battle!
